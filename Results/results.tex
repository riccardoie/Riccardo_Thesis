\chapter{Results}\label{chap:results}
This chapter presents the results of running METIS with NVOL. Throughout this chapter we will compare these results to METIS own total communication volume objective (as well as other partitioners?). To generate the graphs and plots in this chapter the tools described in Chapter~\ref{chap:NVOL tooling} were used. These results show the impact of applying a partition-aware objective function and compare whether this leads to any improvements in the resulting partitioning.
